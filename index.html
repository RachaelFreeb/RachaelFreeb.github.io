Rachael Freebourn

Rachael Freebourn, 21, received her BA in Film & Media from San Francisco State University. She will work anywhere but currently resides in San Francisco. Her life long passion for storytelling and directing has found a home in her unique film style and vision of the world. She is skilled in performative work, portraiture, short form media, and other areas. Rachael and her creative works are prepared to produce the greatest output for you, whatever your creative demands may be. Please use the contact tab to make a reservation or contact for any reason.
